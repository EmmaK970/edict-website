Making Clinical Trials Work for Everyone

Clinical trials are the foundation of evidence-based medicine, yet they consistently fail to include the full diversity of patient populations. This limits the applicability of research findings and perpetuates health inequities.

EDICT brings together leading universities, research institutions, and industry partners across Europe to address this critical challenge through innovative doctoral training.

Standardised EDI Metrics

Developing core outcome sets for measuring diversity in trials

AI-Powered Solutions

Real-time bias detection and inclusive recruitment tools

Pan-European Impact

Training researchers across 8 countries with diverse expertise

Our Research Pillars

Four interconnected work packages driving systemic change in clinical trial inclusivity

01

Measurement & Metrics

Developing standardised approaches to measure and report EDI in clinical trials.

02

Innovative Methodologies

Creating new methods for trial design that enhance representation.

03

Implementation Tools

Building practical resources including training modules and ethical frameworks.

04

Systemic Change

Driving lasting change through stakeholder engagement and patient partnership.
