Making Clinical Trials Work for Everyone

Clinical trials are the foundation of evidence-based healthcare, testing whether new treatments actually work. However, too often, the people in these studies don't reflect the diversity of patients who will eventually use them. This means treatments may not work as well for everyone.

EDICT brings together leading universities, research institutions, and industry partners across Europe, working together to make clinical trials more inclusive.

Inclusive Research Standards

Building the foundations for equitable trial design, measurement, and evaluation

Innovative Methodologies

Advancing new tools and approaches for inclusive trial design

Pan-European Impact

Training researchers across seven European universities and institutions with diverse expertise
